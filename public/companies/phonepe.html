How PhonePe Interviews for AI/ML Roles

Large-scale digital-payments platform; ML/DS roles span fraud, credit/risk, recommendations, and growth analytics on massive transaction data. The loop is coding-heavy (DSA, SQL) with strong statistics and applied ML depth, plus a hiring-manager round.

The Interview Loop

  1. Online coding test — Elimination round, typically ~3 coding problems over 80–90 minutes; for DS roles often split into ~30 min SQL and ~30 min technical/stats.
  2. Technical interview 1 — Coding and DSA to assess implementation skill and problem-solving logic (60–90 min, medium–hard).
  3. Technical interview 2 — Deeper technical round — DSA/DP, programming-language and systems fundamentals; for DS, ML and statistics depth (statistics can be rated hard).
  4. Project / case discussion — Deep-dive into past projects and applied case studies relevant to payments/fintech.
  5. HR / hiring manager — Behavioral and culture-fit questions plus company-specific values (45–70 min).

What They Weight

  • Strong DSA and coding under time pressure
  • High bar on statistics for data-science roles
  • SQL fluency on large transaction datasets
  • Applied ML tied to fraud, risk, and growth

Focus Your Prep On

  • Data structures, algorithms, and dynamic programming
  • SQL and query optimization
  • Statistics and probability (rated demanding)
  • Applied ML for fraud/risk and recommendations
  • Systems / OS fundamentals for engineering roles

Prep angle: Drill DSA and SQL heavily, and — for DS roles — go deep on statistics; prepare project case studies framed around payments fraud, risk, or growth metrics.
